TECHNICAL ABSTRACT (Limit 2000 characters, approximately 200 words)
ASTS is pleased to present this proposal to demonstrate the feasibility of using selective laser melting (SLM) to process commercially-available, low-cost materials in the manufacture of an advanced bipropellant thruster. The refractory matrix composite (RMC) exhibits exceptional high-temperature strength, which will reduce our thruster's mass over state of the art C-103 chambers. It should also reduce fuel film cooling requirements, so thruster performance will benefit significantly.
