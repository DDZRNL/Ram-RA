Technical Abstract (Limit 200 words) Synergistic materials and multifunctional structures fabrication technologies will be developed, evaluated, and intergrated to enhance and enable the design and operation of next generation ultralightweight space telescopes: (1) membrane reflectors comprised of preformed piezoelectric film laminated with preformed seamless polyimide films; (2) preformed piezoelectric polyimide reflector films; (3) active non-contact reflector membrane shape control using an electron gun; and (4) deployable preformed thin polyimide film reflector support structure elements and assemblies that have sufficient inherent stiffness or can be permanently rigidized with lightweight structural foam. All four technologies are scaleable and can provide significant increase in reflector surface accuracy and reduction in cost and weight of large telescope systems. 8. Potential Commercial Application(s)(Limit 200 words) This work can lead to demonstrated practical breakthroughs in large-scale ultralightweight highly accurate thin film reflectors for solar thermal rocket concentrators, large antennas, microwave concentrators, next generation telescopes, and solar conccentrators for electrical power generation and high temperature materials processing in space. The technology is also applicable to smart switches, sensors, and transducers that respond to stimuli such as heat, light or electric and magnetic fields.
