Technical Abstract (Limit 200 words) Design and construct a space capable laser transmitter with 100 mJ/pulse, 3-30 ns pulse width, 40 to 100 Hz repetition rate, with 3 pm resolution and >5% overall efficiency. 8. Potential Commercial Application(s)(Limit 200 words) The laser will be of considerable interest to many military and industrial consumers. On the military front, there are numerous tools, such as the Airborne Laser and various lidar programs, that require a laser such as this that has high energy, high brightness, very efficient and small/light-weight. In the industrial sector, a 100 Hz laser is not in as much demand. However, the lessons learned in this effort are directly applicable to the classical industrial laser system. In the medical arena, there are applications for a 100 Hz system, and in this instance, compactness, high brightness are key features. In addition, the lessons learned in this effort should be, at least in part, transferable to very common medical and industrial laser systems which are usually CW pumped.
