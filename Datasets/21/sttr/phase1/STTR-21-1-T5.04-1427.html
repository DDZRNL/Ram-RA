Technical Abstract (Limit 2000 characters, approximately 200 words):

The objective of this proposal is to overcome significant challenges towards a deployable quantum repeater in a future quantum internet. We will demonstrate a path towards a cost-efficient quantum repeater node that integrates diamond color centers (CCs) as quantum memories with a photonic integrated circuit (PIC) in a compact cryogenic system.

          
          
     
Potential NASA Applications (Limit 1500 characters, approximately 150 words):

NASA has initiated several efforts towards both space- and ground-based quantum networks, including the National Space Quantum Laboratory, involvement under the INQNET program, and an upcoming effort under “Marconi 2.0”.

          
          
     
Potential Non-NASA Applications (Limit 1500 characters, approximately 150 words):

Our proposed work will greatly extend the entanglement distance for a ground-based quantum internet towards the metropolitan scale of 100+ km through the successful development of a quantum repeater.
