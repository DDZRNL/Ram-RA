PROPOSAL NUMBER: S4.01-9429 (For NASA Use Only - Chron: 012570 )
PROPOSAL TITLE:
Nanofabricated GC

TECHNICAL ABSTRACT (LIMIT 200 WORDS)

A nanofabricated, palm-sized gas chromatography (GC) is proposed for the fast, in-situ, analysis of chemicals in space. A prototype miniature GC column-oven subsystem will be designed, nanofabricated and tested in this Phase I to prove the technical feasibility.

POTENTIAL COMMERCIAL APPLICATIONS
The proposed innovation will readily fit the following applications if proved successful:

? Low cost, in-situ, monitoring of pollutants in air, water and soil
? Warning against chemical attacks
? Detection of explosives and illegal drugs
? Process monitoring and control
. Space exploration
