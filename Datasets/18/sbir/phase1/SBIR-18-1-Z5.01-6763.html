Technical Abstract

The proposed research is aimed at investigating the feasibility to provide an Augmented Reality (AR) based support platform for Human-Robots collaboration. The envisioned concept provides AR interface to help astronauts leverage Robots collaborators during procedure execution. There are multiple potential use cases to Human-Robots collaboration such as co-presence where human and robots are working side by side, collaborating on the execution of procedures and sharing the same spatial area, or remote control of the robots by a human within or outside of a spacecraft, or on a planetary surface. These human-robots collaboration examples can benefit from the use of AR in order to increase the overall situational awareness.

Potential NASA Applications

Potential NASA applications:
•    Man – Robots space mission including SPHERE  and Robonaut on board the ISS
•    Planetary surface activities
•    Deep Space Gateway mission
•    Security, Disaster Relief & Support
•    Complex EVA activities with Robots support

 

Potential Non-NASA Applications

Potential Non-NASA applications:
•    Complex and expensive maintenance tasks, which need to be performed safely by joint man-robots team
•    Robots for homes, hospitals, and offices interacting with humans for their tasks
